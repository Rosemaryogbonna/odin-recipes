Tomato sauce

This tomato sauce is my go to easy fix dinner. I usually have this with white rice or pasta and by God it is the best thing you could eat.

What you will need;

  • 5 large tomatoes
  • 3 habanero peppers
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tsp dried ground ginger
  • 1 tsp All all-purpose spice
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable oil or any oil of your choice

Method;

  1. Chop up the tomatoes, pepper, onion and garlic.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat up the oil for 1 minute.
  3. Add onions and garlic and fry till the onions become translucent.
  4. Add tomatoes and pepper and fry for 3 minutes.
  5. When the oil starts to float on top, add spices and seasoning. Add salt to taste.
  6. Cook for an additional 2 minutes and turn off the stove.
  7. Serve with boiled white rice and enjoy!
